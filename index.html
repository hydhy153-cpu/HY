Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a site plan for a building permit?

Yes, most building projects require a site plan for permit approval. HYSitePlan.com creates permit-ready site plans that are accepted by local authorities nationwide. Our detailed drawings show your property boundaries, structures, setbacks, and all required elements for successful permit applications.

How quickly can I get a site plan?

HYSitePlan.com delivers most site plans within 24 hours, ensuring your project stays on schedule. Our streamlined process and experienced team can handle rush orders to meet tight permit deadlines. We understand local requirements across all states and create compliant drawings quickly.

What types of site plans does HYSitePlan.com offer?

HYSitePlan.com offers multiple site plan options including Basic Site Plans, Detailed Site Plans with structures and landscaping, Medium Site Plans, Commercial Site Plans, and our comprehensive “The Works” package. Each plan type meets different local requirements and project scales nationwide.

Are HYSitePlan.com drawings accepted by local authorities?

Yes, HYSitePlan.com drawings are widely accepted by permit offices across the United States. Our site plans meet or exceed local building department standards and include all required elements such as property boundaries, setbacks, existing structures, and utility locations as mandated by local regulations.

How much does a site plan cost?

Site plan costs vary by project complexity. HYSitePlan.com offers transparent pricing starting with Basic plans, Medium complexity plans, Detailed plans with full landscaping, and Commercial options. Visit our products page to see current pricing with no hidden fees.

What information do I need to provide for my site plan?

For your site plan, provide your property address, any existing surveys or plot plans, photos of your property, and details about planned improvements. HYSitePlan.com handles the research for property boundaries and local requirements specific to your area.

Can HYSitePlan.com help with commercial projects?

Absolutely! HYSitePlan.com specializes in commercial site plans for businesses nationwide. Our commercial drawings meet stringent local requirements, include parking layouts, accessibility compliance, and all elements required by local commercial building codes. We handle retail, office, industrial, and mixed-use developments.

Do you provide revisions for site plans?

Yes, HYSitePlan.com includes revisions with all site plans. If local authorities request changes or you need modifications, we’ll update your drawings at no additional cost. Our team understands common revision requests and works quickly to keep your project moving forward.

Save Time And Money With My Site Plan

Our plans are for those whose building department DOES NOT require a Surveyor, Engineer, or Architect Stamp. This is not a Legal Survey, nor is it intended to be or replace a Legal Survey. We create your site plan drawing so you don’t have to.

Instead of physically visiting your location, we use third party data. This includes satellite imagery, GIS information, county parcel maps, and client input (survey, sketch, ground measurements etc.). We also rely on other public information sources to deliver professional, scaled plans.

We are the solution for anyone who doesn’t have the time or ability to create their own plan.
